## Suggested Learning Order

1. **Start with:** Simplilearn "Reinforcement Learning Explained" (15 min overview)
2. **Then:** StatQuest "Reinforcement Learning" + "Q-Learning" videos
3. **Code along:** Sentdex RL Python tutorial OR CodeBasics RL tutorial
4. **Fun project:** Nicholas Renotte "RL Flappy Bird" (learn by building a game AI)
5. **Go deeper:** David Silver lectures 1-3 on YouTube
6. **Course:** HuggingFace Deep RL Course (free, modern, hands-on)

---

> **Tip:** Reinforcement learning is BEST learned by building game-playing agents. Don't just watch videos — follow along and train your own agent to play Flappy Bird, CartPole, or Atari games. Seeing your agent improve from random to skillful is the best way to understand RL.
