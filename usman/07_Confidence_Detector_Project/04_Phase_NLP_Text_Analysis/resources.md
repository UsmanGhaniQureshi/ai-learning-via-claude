# Resources — NLP & Text Analysis

## NLP Fundamentals
- NLTK documentation — https://www.nltk.org/ (Python NLP toolkit)
- spaCy documentation — https://spacy.io/ (modern NLP library)
- Regex tutorial — https://regexone.com/ (pattern matching fundamentals)

## Filler Word Research
- Clark & Fox Tree (2002) — "Using uh and um in spontaneous speaking" — the definitive paper on WHY humans use fillers
- Toastmasters "Ah Counter" role — how professional speaking organizations count fillers

## Speech Analysis
- Words Per Minute research — optimal presentation pace is 130-160 WPM (National Speakers Association)
- Hedging in academic writing — Hyland (1998) — understanding when hedging is appropriate vs inappropriate
